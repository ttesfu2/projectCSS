To find perfect place to have Ethiopian Bakery and Coffe is Guna Buna. Guna Buna is an ethiopian bakery and coffee house established in 2015. We honor and continue the old tradition of fresh ingridients, we could not get over how delicious the coffee was and hot readily available it was as well. Buna is the traditional Ethiopian style of coffee, prepared in a clay pot known as a jebena, and served in small espresso cups. Other types of European style coffees, like macchiatos, are also available in Guna Buna.
